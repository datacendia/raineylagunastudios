## 04 · Signature mechanics on `raineylagunastudios.com`

These are running software. Each one is a world-first, or close to it, for a design-studio homepage.

### 04.1 The Living Manifesto
- File: `index.html` inline script + `--type-wght`, `--type-opsz`, `--type-tracking` CSS vars.
- Source: Open-Meteo API, Lima coordinates, refresh every 10 minutes.
- Humidity drives **wght** (240–520). Cloud cover drives **opsz** (10–24). Wind drives **tracking**.
- Same paragraph reads differently at different hours.

### 04.2 The Hydroprint Lab
- File: `scripts/hydroprint-lab.js`.
- Three.js WebGL ceramic mug. Five house patterns. User upload. Five ceramic base colours. Matte / Satin / Gloss.
- Orbit to inspect. Auto-rotate resumes 4 s after release.
- "Make this object" routes to WhatsApp with context.

### 04.3 Reverse Commissioning
- File: `scripts/reverse-commissioning.js`.
- Visitor types business name + category. Receives a typeset one-page brief on ivory paper: observation → diagnosis → three-phase program → investment → timeline.
- v0.2 is deterministic templating over six categories (café, restaurant, fitness, wellness, boutique, school).
- v0.3 plan: wire to Claude + read Vigía signals for specific observations.

### 04.4 The Object That Remembers You (NFC)
- File: `scripts/memory-object.js`.
- Every Studios object ships with an embedded NFC chip. Tap with phone → private memory page: order date, Lima weather that day, manifesto typography that afternoon, fabrication log, ship date.
- v0.2 is a clickable demo. v1 needs the NFC provisioning pipeline in the workshop.

### 04.5 Proof of Fabrication
- Files: `scripts/proof-of-fabrication.js` + `verify.html` + `data/proofs/*.json`.
- Every object also ships with a printed QR → public verification page with Ed25519 signature over `serial | cad_file_hash | finished_at | public_key`.
- v0.3 status: UI shipped, signature cosmetic. Real verification lands when Stuart generates an offline keypair on USB.

### 04.6 The Fabrication Twin
- Files: `scripts/twin-wall.js` + `data/twins.json`.
- Every object is made twice. One ships, one joins a 48-cell wall in the workshop. Homepage renders the wall as a live mosaic with hover-metadata.
- Monthly ritual: photograph wall, update `data/twins.json`.
- 48 cells = ~year one. Rebuild to 192 cells once full.

### 04.7 Source-Code Commons (policy, not software)
- `COMMONS.md` at repo root.
- Every client website goes public on `github.com/raineylaguna-commons` from day one with consent.
- Applies to both Studios web projects and Rainey Laguna `.com` projects.

### 04.8 The Hydroprint Lab — full configurator _(shipped 3 May 2026)_
- §04.2 was a viewer only. v0.4 is a real configurator: object-type selector (mug · vase · plate · tile · custom), quantity stepper, live price computation, and a spec list (pattern, base colour, finish, qty, unit + total in PEN).
- The "Make this object" button writes the full configuration into the WhatsApp deep-link so the conversation starts with a quote, not a question.
- Implementation: `scripts/hydroprint-lab.js` (3D) + inline `wireLabControls()` in `index.html` (pricing + WA handoff).

### 04.9 Reverse Commissioning — live-drop UI _(shipped 3 May 2026)_
- §04.3 was a brief generator. The live-drop layer sits **above** it on the page.
- File: `scripts/reverse-drops.js` reading `data/drops.json`.
- Renders upcoming / live / closed drops with countdowns to the minute, claimable pieces (numbered editions, prices), waitlist toggle when sold out.
- Each drop has 3–8 pieces, each with its own serial that links forward to a future `/memory/<serial>/` page once shipped.
- The brief generator below stays — drops are the front door for buyers, briefs are the front door for places.

### 04.10 Public Memory page _(shipped 3 May 2026)_
- §04.4 was a homepage demo. v1 is a standalone public page at `/memory/<serial>/`.
- File: `memory/index.html` reads `?s=DEMO-0001` (or `<serial>`) from the URL and pulls the matching `data/proofs/<serial>.json`.
- Renders: order date, Lima weather that day, manifesto typography that afternoon, fabrication log, ship date, Ed25519 seal, link back to `/verify.html?serial=...`.
- This is the page NFC tags deep-link to. URL shape is **stable**: do not refactor.

### 04.11 Marca · brand management practice _(shipped 3 May 2026)_
- New page at `/marca/` documenting eight brand-management mechanics, none of which exist on any other studio site we've seen:
  1. **Brand Provenance Chain** — every brand asset Ed25519-signed, public canonical / deprecated / unknown ledger at `data/brand/asset-manifest.json`.
  2. **Logo Stress-Test Wall** — homepage shows the wordmark surviving 12 hostile contexts (low contrast, blur, distortion, overprint, single colour…).
  3. **Color Witness** — every brand colour ships with the climate of the day it was approved (`data/brand/color-witness.json`).
  4. **Two-Year Letter** — every Identidad engagement ends with a sealed letter to the client's future self, opened on the second anniversary.
  5. **Brand Shadow** — quarterly screenshot of where the client's brand actually appears in the wild (Google, IG, neighbours' menus) vs where it should.
  6. **Naming Q&A** — public archive of naming decisions and the questions that shaped them.
  7. **Brand Heartbeat** — a single rhythm metric per client (post cadence vs voice consistency) on the Care monthly report.
  8. **Voice Calibrator** — visitor pastes a sentence; Studios scores it against the brand voice. Live tool, not a deck.
- The homepage chapter index is renumbered: `01 Manifesto / 02 Lab / 03 Marca / 04 Comisión / 05 Memoria / 06 Pruebas / 07 Gemelos / 08 Tienda` (Marca slotted at 03).

## 05 · Voice and tone

### 05.1 Voice in one paragraph

> Studios writes like a maker who has made the thing and is now inviting you to hold it. Shorter than a poet, plainer than an architect, more specific than a gallerist. Spanish primary, English second. Dry humour. Never describes the work; describes the _decision_ behind the work. Verbs over adjectives. Materials over adjectives. Dates, weights, dimensions, batches — these are our adjectives.

### 05.2 Rules (non-negotiable)

- **Spanish primary, English second.** `es-PE` is default. English is the mirror.
- **Never use the word "craft" in English copy.** Use "made," "fabricated," "printed," "cut."
- **Never use "bespoke," "curated," "handcrafted," "artisanal," "luxury."** Ban list.
- **Never use emoji.** Ever.
- **Never use the words "AI," "blockchain," "NFT."** Even for Proof of Fabrication — say "firma criptográfica" or "Ed25519."
- **Numbers are specific.** 320 ml not "medium-sized." 220 g not "heavy." 0.08 mm layer not "fine."
- **Every object has a name.** Not a SKU. A name.
- **Prices are public and shown in anchor form.** "Desde S/ 80." No hidden pricing.
- **Bilingual switching is instantaneous** — every visible string has both `data-es` and `data-en`.

### 05.3 Words we like

> taller · horno · pieza · ficha · objeto · superficie · acabado · tacto · archivo · lote · cédula · colofón · tirada · temple · grano · clima · garúa · hilo · raíz · próxima · casa · manos

### 05.4 Tone shifts

- **Manifesto:** aphoristic, low-light, slightly cold.
- **Hydroprint Lab:** technical, affectionate with materials.
- **Reverse Commissioning:** authoritative, observational.
- **Memory Object:** intimate, whispered.
- **Proof / Twin:** factual, forensic.

## 08 · Operational rules

### 08.1 Fabrication discipline
- **Every object gets signed.** No exceptions. If the keypair is inaccessible for a day, objects wait.
- **Every object gets twinned.** Only exemptions: jobs under S/ 500 where client declines the twin surcharge.
- **Every object gets photographed on the wall monthly.** Update `data/twins.json` the same day.
- **Every object's CAD file hash is computed at fabrication time** and baked into the proof JSON.
- **Every hydroprint job logs:** pattern name, film batch, activator, cure time, tank temperature.

### 08.2 Keys and secrets
- The Ed25519 signing key lives on a hardware USB held by Stuart. Never committed. Never copied to cloud.
- Public key is baked into `verify.html` and the README.
- If the key is ever lost, rotate to a new key; old certificates remain verifiable against the old public key (which stays on record).

### 08.3 Response SLAs
- WhatsApp: same-day.
- Email: 24 hours, weekdays.
- Fabrication lead time quoted per job; default 7–14 days.

### 08.4 Contracts
- Every commission includes: scope, materials, fabrication window, delivery address, whether Twin applies, whether Proof of Fabrication is free-tier or paid, consent for public photography of the twin.
- Law 29733 applies to client data.
- Commons Clause (see `COMMONS.md`) opt-in for any website work.

### 08.5 Non-competes
- Studios will not produce identical hydroprint patterns for two hospitality clients in the same neighbourhood.
- Studios will not produce brand-overlap for two clients in the same category within 12 months.
